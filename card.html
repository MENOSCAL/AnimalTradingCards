Turtle

The Galapagos tortoise is the only species of green turtle found in the archipelago. Normally, every 2 or 3 years, the females return to the same beach to lay their eggs.

  • Name: Galapagos green turtle
  • Scientific Name: Chelonia mydas agassisi
  • Maximum Length: 84 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 80 years

Galapagos green sea turtles are reptiles very well adapted to life in the sea and are found in tropical or subtropical waters of the Pacific Ocean; It is considered the second largest species of turtle in the world.
